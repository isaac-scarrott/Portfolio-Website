First thoughts on first SD job
05/07/2019
Well my first thoughts on my new job is that software development in the industry is completely different to software development at university! I thought it was a small smooth transition from university to work life with me knowing a decent amount about what I was doing but no, it was a real punch in the face and especially during the first few days a very daunting learning experience. The code base, the new languages, new tools, meeting new people, I won’t lie, it was a lot to take in and felt a bit overwhelmed. But I managed it and am now feel I am settling in well to the company and understanding the (huge) code base a lot more. Slowly but surely I’m getting there.

I’ll go a little bit into the tech stack of the company. We use Javascript, PHP, React, Kendo UI (ugh), JQuery, Node.js, Symfony, Doctrine and Oracle. Some of these I know decently well some I have never used (Kendo UI (ugh again)). Overall these are good languages, however Python will always have a special place in my heart. First impressions are the company culture is very good and I like the direction they are headed and definitely want to be a part of it for at least the next couple of years (maybe even doing a part time masters as well who knows...).

On another note from work I have also started the coursera Deep Learning Specialization from deeplearning.ai and the man himself Andrew Ng, to build upon the foundations I have laid down at university in my final year. Also on another tangent I GOT A FIRST AT UNIVERSITY. It is so surrell and I can’t really believe it (I can believe it because of the amount of hard work and sleepless nights I have had to ensure). This has really set the foundation now and I really can build upon this now to advance in my career.

On a final note I have also come up with (what I think is) a great app idea that hasn’t really been done before. However it could be the app that the (especially male) youth of today never knew they needed. Not going to write too much about this on here as this is for another post, nor will I start it yet as I don’t want to put too much on my plate at once.

Anyway here is the second blog post done still don’t know why I’m doing these but I will start to do them more regularly and see how they develop.
